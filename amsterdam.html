PROJECT: FINDING AN IDEAL LOCATION IN AMSTERDAM

Context

A pregnant friend was moving to Amsterdam and to help her, I decided to provide locations and specific zipcodes where it would be ideal to live.

Criteria

I came up with a criteria for zipcodes that would probably make the pregancy as convenient as possible:

  • within 500 metres from Bus Stops
  • within 250 metres from Parks
  • within 1 kilometre from Hospitals

Additional Files

Aside from these variables, I need the additional files:

  • Zipcodes
  • Quarters

All files are downloaded from the Amsterdam City website

Steps and Results

Buffer zones were created based on the criteria that was set for the three variables.

These were then 'merged' together by only selecting areas that intersect the buffer zones.

This merged polygon, we call it 'good zones', was then used to select zipcodes if the zipcodes happen to intersect these good zones.

Upon looking closely at the map, some areas seem to be too far from the city centre. Using the districts, I only selected zipcodes that fall within the inner city districts.

I presented the two maps to her as reference, and even provided an Excel file of zipcodes for her to use when she was doing her apartment search.
